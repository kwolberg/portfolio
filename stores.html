Welcome!

Semicolon Bookstore

Semicolon Bookstore is located in Chicago, IL. It is Chicago's only Black woman-owned independent bookstore and gallery space. Semicolon Bookstore aims to create a welcoming space for their readers. Semicolon Bookstore has a '#ClearTheShelves' initiative where they invite Chicago Public School students to the store every month to take home books free of charge.

Harriett's Books

Named after Harriett Tubman, Harriett's Books in Philadelphia, PA celebrates female activists and authors. this Philadelphia bookshop celebrates female activists and authors. The store is also running a program, called "Essentials for Essentials" where customers can purchase books that go directly to essential workers fighting the coronavirus.

Literati Bookstore

Located right in downtown Ann Arbor is our very own Literati Bookstore. Literati is an independent bookstore that has a variety of books, and a variety of events - including author readings, book clubs, and poetry nights. I love Literati; and if you are in the Ann Arbor area you should definitely check out this store!
